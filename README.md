# Household Food Security & Vulnerability Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes household-level food security, economic stability, and vulnerability to external shocks (conflict and price fluctuations) across various states and regions in Myanmar. Using **Power BI**, I developed an interactive dashboard to visualize key humanitarian and socio-economic indicators, helping to identify at-risk populations and regional disparities.

The analysis spans data from 2020 to 2024, providing insights into how demographic factors and external crises impact household nutrition and economic resilience.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- **Data Visualization & Modeling:** Power BI (`.pbix`)
- **Data Source:** Structured CSV Dataset (`Dataset.csv`)
- **Key Concepts:** Data Cleaning, DAX (Data Analysis Expressions), Relational Data Modeling, Interactive Dashboards, Humanitarian Data Analysis.

---

## 📊 The Dataset

The dataset contains thousands of household surveys detailing demographics, economic status, and standard food security metrics. Key features include:

### Geographic & Demographic Data:
- `StateRegion` & `Township` (e.g., Rakhine, Mandalay, Chin)
- `UrbanRural` classification
- `HHSize` (Household Size) and `ChildrenUnder5`
- `FemaleHeaded` household indicator

### Food Security Indicators:
- `FCS` & `FCSCategory` (Food Consumption Score: Acceptable, Borderline, Poor)
- `HungerScore` & `HungerCategory`
- `DietDiversityScore` & `DietDiversityCategory`

### Socio-Economic & Vulnerability Metrics:
- `WealthQuintile` (Poorest to Richest)
- `MonthlyIncome` & `MonthlyFoodExpenditure`
- `ConflictShock` & `PriceShock` (Exposure to external crises)
- `IDPStatus` (Internally Displaced Persons)

---

## 🎯 Key Objectives & Insights

This Power BI dashboard was built to answer critical questions such as:

1. **Impact of Shocks:** How do conflict and price shocks correlate with a decline in Food Consumption Scores (FCS)?
2. **Regional Vulnerability:** Which States/Regions experience the highest rates of severe hunger and lowest diet diversity?
3. **Economic Disparities:** What is the relationship between Wealth Quintiles and monthly food expenditure?
4. **Demographic Risks:** Are female-headed households or households with a high number of children under 5 more susceptible to food insecurity?
